Exploring Artificial Intelligence for Cybersecurity

by Michael Folayan

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the landscape of cybersecurity by introducing intelligent systems capable of detecting and responding to threats faster than traditional methods. From anomaly detection to automated incident response, AI enhances defense mechanisms against evolving cyber threats. As cyberattacks become more complex, the integration of machine learning models and AI algorithms offers proactive protection, especially for high-risk industries like finance and healthcare.

This area of research is critical in building resilient digital infrastructure. AI for cybersecurity not only strengthens existing protocols but also opens new frontiers for innovation and safety in the cyber realm.
